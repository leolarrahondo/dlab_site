About d.Lab Software

We are a team of Developers and Engineers coming from a variety of industry backgrounds ranging from Life Sciences to Department of Defense. We have over 30 years of combined experience in the IT industry, during which we've learned the right way to make software.

We specialize in analyzing a business and identifying where cutting-edge technology can streamline its process. We believe that technology such as Google Glass, touchscreen devices, and cloud data management services can be a reality for any business regardless of size, and at an affordable price.

We founded d.Lab because we are passionate about taking emerging technology and using it to make businesses better. As such, we strive to be a great business that makes great software for great people.
